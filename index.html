Glyptodon Fossil Sites Map

Interactive visualization of Pleistocene glyptodont fossil locations across the Americas, highlighting sites with potential human interaction evidence.

About the Data

This map displays fossil sites of the extinct genus Glyptodon, a giant armadillo-like creature that lived during the Pleistocene epoch (approx. 2.5 million to 11,700 years ago).

Each marker represents a fossil site. Click on a marker to see details about the site, including the species and estimated age.
